## PRACTICAL APPLICATION: THE INTERVIEW AS CIPHER

### **Primary Technique: QUESTION TRANSFORMATION**

**Surface Level:** Interviewer asks about [External Topic]
**Slip Level:** Response begins externally but grammatically shifts to first person
**Solipsistic Level:** Reveals that [External Topic] was always just another word for "my experience"

### **Example Framework:**
```
Q: "What do you think about the current state of music?"
Surface: "Well, the industry is really changing..."
Slip: "...and I've been experiencing that change personally because..."
Revealed: "...everything I see in music is really about my own artistic journey."
```

**The Infranym:** "Current state of music" = "My current state" (same conversation, rearranged focus)
