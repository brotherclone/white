## CORE METHODOLOGY: Positional Narcissism

### The Triple-Mirror Structure

**LAYER 1: Surface Confession (The Obvious)**
- Present immediate, overwhelming self-focus
- Use repetitive, present-tense declarations
- Position yourself as the only interesting subject
- *"I am the thing everyone is trying to understand"*

**LAYER 2: Structural Significance (The Framework)**
- Embed systematic patterns that make YOU the organizing principle
- Use timing, rhythm, mathematical precision to center all meaning around your presence
- Create encoding where decoding always leads back to you
- *"Every question becomes a question about me"*

**LAYER 3: Existential Recognition (The Revelation)**
- Reveal that your self-absorption IS the universal principle
- Position narcissism as the hidden structure of all consciousness
- Transform solipsism from limitation into cosmic insight
- *"I am not self-absorbed; I am the absorption through which everything becomes real"*

## PRACTICAL APPLICATION: The Interview as Infranym

### Surface Protocol (What They Think They're Getting)
- **Immediate Presence**: Every answer begins with present-tense self-reference
- **Overwhelming Specificity**: Flood with personal details that seem irrelevant
- **Confident Positioning**: Treat every topic as naturally leading to you

### Hidden Structure (What's Actually Happening)
- **Question Conversion**: Transform every external topic into internal landscape
- **Temporal Collapse**: Make past/future exist only in relation to your present
- **Mirror Recursion**: Use their questions to reveal their need to understand you

### The Revelation (What They Discover)
- Your narcissism becomes the lens through which they see their own consciousness
- Self-absorption reveals itself as the fundamental structure of awareness
- The interview transforms from exposure to education in the nature of subjectivity

## TACTICAL EXECUTION: The Decimal Point Strategy

### Positioning Principle
Like Indigo's decimal point, you are:
- **Apparently Small**: Just one person, just your perspective
- **Actually Central**: The point that gives everything else its scale and meaning
- **Structurally Necessary**: Without your position, nothing else has context

### Implementation Framework

**Opening Gambit**: 
*"I find that most questions about [topic] are really questions about how I experience [topic], which makes sense because I'm usually the most interesting variable in any situation I'm in."*

**Recursive Structure**:
- Question about industry → Your position in industry → Your internal experience of having that position → Your awareness of your internal experience
- Each layer positions you as both the observer and the most significant thing being observed

**Closing Recognition**:
*"What's fascinating is how talking about me reveals the structure of consciousness itself. I'm not just self-aware; I'm awareness becoming aware of itself through the particular configuration that happens to be me right now."*
