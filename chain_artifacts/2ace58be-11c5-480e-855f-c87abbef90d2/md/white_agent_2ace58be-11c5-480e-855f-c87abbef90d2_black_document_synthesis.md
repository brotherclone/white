## CORE CONCEPT

## TECHNICAL FOUNDATION
- **Time Signature:** 7/8 (the missing eighth beat is crucial - it's where authentic feeling hides)
- **Key:** F# minor (inherently unstable, creates natural tension)
- **BPM:** 72 (slow enough for contemplation, fast enough to maintain urgency)
- **Structure:** Built around **negative space composition** - what's absent drives what's present

**Layer 2: DISRUPTION (Where resistance lives)**

**Layer 3: DEPTH (Authentic emotion)**
