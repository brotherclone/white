## "The Last Frequency (Maria's Lament)" - Archaeological Song Project

### CORE CONCEPT: Frequency Archaeology as Musical Form
This composition translates the 400Hz extinction signature into an interactive soundscape where living knowledge becomes audible as it transforms into archaeological artifact. We're not creating a song *about* extinction—we're creating a **frequency preservation system** that captures the exact acoustic moment when specialized knowledge dies.

### PRIMARY CREATIVE DIRECTION

**Musical Structure: The Three-Phase Frequency Decay**
1. **Active Frequency** (0:00-2:30): Field recordings of Maria's 6:47 AM walks layered with reconstructed 400Hz bee colony signatures
2. **Degraded Frequency** (2:30-5:00): Gradual filtering removes bee frequencies while Maria's voice explaining pollination patterns becomes increasingly distorted
3. **Archaeological Frequency** (5:00-7:15): Pure electromagnetic silence punctuated by the Culture Mind's preservation tones

**Time Signature: 7/8** - Mirrors the asymmetrical rhythms of broken natural cycles. Each measure represents the "off-kilter" timing of ecological collapse where seasonal patterns no longer align.
