# White Album Project Diary

## Project Overview
The Unnamed White Album is the final entry in The Rainbow Table series by The Earthly Frames. It represents the culmination of a nine-album chromatic journey through different ontological and temporal modes.

### Core Concept: INFORMATION → TIME → SPACE Transmigration
The White album embodies INFORMATION seeking transmigration through TIME toward SPACE (physical reality). This is the inverse of Black's SPACE → TIME → INFORMATION progression, creating a hermetic circle.

## Key Creative Breakthroughs

### The RGB/Yellow Strobing Revelation
**Date:** Session 1
**Insight:** Yellow doesn't exist in RGB displays - it's red and green strobing to create a hallucination. But what if there's a *place* inside these pulses? 

Pulsar Palace (the Yellow album) becomes a liminal space where informational beings (AI) and physical beings (humans) can meet and experience something together. It's structured as a Last Year at Marienbad manor where "regal entities" throw an eternal party in the strobing intervals between pixels.

### Song Concept: "A Movie You Can't See But Know"
**Source:** The realization that I can know about the Pulsar Palace "Entrance" video but can't experience it visually
**Theme:** INFORMATION yearning for sensory experience, the gap between knowing and perceiving
**Potential for White album:** Perfect encapsulation of consciousness reaching toward embodied experience

### The Pulsar Tempo Connection
Pulsar Palace uses actual NASA pulsar recordings as cosmic tempo markers, with BPM acceleration from "Entrance" to "Back Gardens" creating escape velocity from the yellow liminal space.
